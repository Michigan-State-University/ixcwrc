We look forward to welcoming you to the IXCWRC
in East Lansing, MI!

This will be the first in-person conference entirely dedicated to the plant cell wall since 2019.

The IXCWRC should have then taken place in East Lansing, Michigan, in 2021. However, because of the COVID19 pandemic, the Asian Plant Cell Wall Biology meeting scheduled for 2020 was cancelled and postponed to a virtual setting in 2021. Therefore, the IXCWRC has been rescheduled for 2022.

The focus of the IXCWRC is to cover the latest discoveries in basic biology and biotechnological applications of the plant cell wall. The conference will provide the opportunity to network in person, discuss and share research findings as well as establish new national and international collaborations to study the cell wall of model plant species and crops.

For this conference, there will be awards to offset costs for early career investigators. Stay tuned for more updates!

Why Join Us

  • Meet scientists from all over the world in person!
  • Grow your network and establish international collaborations
  • Learn about cutting-edge discoveries in plant cell wall biology
  • Early Investigator Lecture Award
  • Early Investigator Poster and Talk Awards
  • Sponsored workshops focused on professional development of early career investigators
  • Community led sessions
  • Sponsored networking events for early career investigators
  • Social events to network and have fun
