Welcome

It is a great honor for us to host the 9th Cell Wall Research Conference (IXCW2022) and we would like to invite you to come to East Lansing and participate the annual conference, which will take place from June 13 - 27, 2022, at the Michigan State University campus in East Lansing, Michigan.

What is Our Goal?

We wish that IXCW2022 will be an academic meeting to discuss the latest development of plant cell wall biology, but also a community gathering to meet with old and new friends colleagues.

  • Structure and function of cell wall polymers
  • Cell wall biosynthesis
  • Cell wall genomics and evolution
  • Cell wall response and plant defense
  • Emerging technologies in cell wall biology and biotechnology
  • Industrial uses of cell wall biomass
